PROFILE PHOTO
=============

Save your photo in THIS folder with the exact name:

    profile.png

(a .jpg also works — just name it profile.jpg and it will still load)

Recommended:
- Square-ish crop, face centered
- Your current black-background photo is ideal — it blends into the dark theme
- Any size works, 500x500px or larger looks crispest

If no photo is present, the site automatically falls back to the "TS" monogram.
